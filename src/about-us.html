This website is mean to create an interactive online entertaining ordering experience for for the Blaze Pizza. It will specifically focus on customizing your own pizza page. It means viewers can design their own pizza based on their own taste. It’s meant to visualize their personalized pizza.

The purpose is to let customers enjoy, have fun and interact with the website while ordering pizza from Blaze Pizza. This web enables customers to visually see how their personalized pizza looks which will incentivize them to purchase pizza from this brand rather than others.

We are targeting all-age range consumers. People who would like to order their customized pizza on Blaze Pizza. Compared to ordering from a delivery app, ordering from blazepizza.com is making them having more fun.
